# FUTURE_DS_01
FUTURE_DS_01 BUSSINESS SALES PERFOMANCE ANALYSIS.
Data Analytics and Data Science task no1.

AUTHOR : MADDA HARSHAVARDHAN REDDY.
An analysis of business sales across multiple regions, categories, sub-categories, segments, revenue and product information.

PROJECT OVERVIEW.
This project aim to analyse business data from 2014-01-03 up to 2017-12-30 in order to gain valuable insights and discover hidden patterns so that we can come up with better recommendations which are crucial for bussiness improvement and informed business decisions.

ANALYSIS REPORT / DASHBOARD.
Our Dasboard will include:

Top Products
Customer Segmentation.
Category analysis
Sales and Revenue trends.
Data visualisation and interpretation.
Regional Analysis.
KEY OBSERVATIONS.
We have Technology as our leading category in sales ($836154.03), followed by furniture ($741999.80), then office supplies ($719047.03).
Technology ($145454.95) is also our lead category in profit, followed by office supplies ( $122490.80) and furniture ($18451.27) being the less profitable.
As we dive deeper we discover that tables are the least profitable sub-category with a whooping $-17725.48 loss in $206965.53 sales. We also discover that copiers are our most profitable sub-category with a profit of $55617.82 followed by phones at $44515.73 and Accessories at $41936.64
We also discover that the West is our leading region followed by the East in both sales and profit, with New Yor City as the best profitable city.
We also discover that the relationship between discount and loss is directly proportional, an increase in discount leads to a dicrease in profit which means an increase in loss.
Profitable transactions avg discount (8.1%) while Loss-making transactions avg discount: 48.1% (6x higher!).
IMMEDIATE INTERVENTION STRATEGIES.
REVISE DISCOUNT STRATEGY: Limit discounts to MAXIMUM 20% on Furniture category. Eliminate discounts >30% on Tables immediately. Create approval workflow for discounts >15%

PRODUCT FOCUS: Increase inventory and marketing for Copiers. Bundle Tables with profitable items (Chairs, Accessories). Review pricing strategy for Tables.

FUTURE RECOMMENDATIONS AND IMPLEMENTATION.
REGIONAL EXPANSION: Replicate West region success in other regions. Investigate what makes New York City successful. Targeted marketing in underperforming cities.

PROFITABILITY OPTIMIZATION: Set profit alert for transactions with <5% margin. Monthly review of loss-making products. Train sales team on profitable upselling.

CUSTOMER SEGMENTATION: Launch Home Office loyalty program (highest profit/customer). Cross-sell Copiers to Corporate segment. Test premium pricing with Home Office segment.
